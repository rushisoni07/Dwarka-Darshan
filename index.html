Temple Timings & Darshan

Get daily Aarti schedule, rush hours, and special puja slots for Dwarkadhish Temple and nearby shrines like Rukmini Devi, Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, and Beyt Dwarka ferries.

How to Reach

Nearest airport: Jamnagar (about 130 km). Rail connectivity from Ahmedabad, Rajkot, and Okha. Frequent state buses operate from major Gujarat cities.

Local Tips

  • Footwear & mobiles may be restricted near sanctum—use cloakrooms.
  • Carry cash for ferries and small vendors.
  • Best months: Oct–Feb; summers are hot & humid.

Taxi & Bus
